Features

Everything you need to care deeply

A thoughtfully designed simulation focused on the joy of nurturing digital life.

Adoption Center

Bring home a new friend. Choose their name and species, and start your journey together with a bond that grows through every interaction.

Health Tracking

Monitor hunger, happiness, energy, and health in real time. Every pet has unique needs, and attentive care keeps them thriving.

Persistent Memories

Your companions are safely stored on your machine. Close the app, come back tomorrow — your pets will be waiting exactly where you left them.

Rich HTTP API

Integrate rspets into your own tools and workflows. Clean REST endpoints make it effortless to build custom dashboards or automation.

Care Actions

How will you spend time together?

Every action matters. Watch your choices shape your companion's mood and wellbeing.

🍖

Feed

A well-fed pet is a happy pet. Restore their hunger levels and give them a small health boost with every nutritious meal you provide.

POST /pets/{id}/feed
🎾

Play

Playtime sparks joy. Increase happiness, burn a little energy, and build an unbreakable bond through fun and games.

POST /pets/{id}/play
🌙

Sleep

Rest is essential for growth. Let your companion nap to recharge their energy and recover precious health points.

POST /pets/{id}/sleep

Ready to meet your new best friend?

Adopting a companion is as simple as a single API call. Give them a name, choose a species, and watch your digital family grow.

shell 
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/pets \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"name":"Momo","species":"cat"}'
