Features

Everything you need to care deeply

A thoughtfully designed simulation focused on the joy of nurturing digital life — now with battles, skills, and elemental types.

Gacha Adoption

Roll for pets backed by species templates. Each has an element, rarity, and unique battle stats. First roll is free!

Health Tracking

Monitor hunger, happiness, energy, health, and cleanliness in real time. Every pet has unique needs, and attentive care keeps them thriving.

Skill System

Pets automatically learn skills as they level up. Equip up to 4 skills for battle — physical, special, or status moves with elemental types.

Turn-Based Battles

Pit your pets against each other in 1v1 combat. Elemental matchups, status conditions, stat stages, and critical hits make every battle unique.

Persistent Memories

Your companions are safely stored in SQLite. Close the app, come back tomorrow — your pets will be waiting exactly where you left them.

Rich HTTP API

Integrate rspets into your own tools and workflows. Clean REST endpoints make it effortless to build custom dashboards or automation.

Care Actions

How will you spend time together?

Every action matters. Watch your choices shape your companion's mood and wellbeing. Train them, explore with them, and keep them healthy.

🍖

Feed

A well-fed pet is a happy pet. Restore their hunger levels and give them a small health boost with every nutritious meal you provide.

POST /pets/{id}/feed
🎾

Play

Playtime sparks joy. Increase happiness, burn a little energy, and build an unbreakable bond through fun and games.

POST /pets/{id}/play
🌙

Sleep

Rest is essential for growth. Let your companion nap to recharge their energy and recover precious health points.

POST /pets/{id}/sleep
🛁

Groom

Keep your companion spotless. Grooming restores cleanliness, boosts happiness, and earns a little experience.

POST /pets/{id}/groom
🏋️

Train

Push your pet to learn new skills. Training costs energy but rewards valuable experience points that lead to level-ups.

POST /pets/{id}/train
🗺️

Explore

Send your pet on a random adventure. They might find hidden trails, learn from the trip, or come back with a few scrapes.

POST /pets/{id}/explore
Battle System

Test your pets in turn-based combat

Pit your companions against each other. Elemental matchups, status effects, and strategic skill choices determine the victor.

⚔️

1v1 Combat

Two pets enter, one leaves victorious. Each turn, both sides select a skill. Speed determines action order.

POST /battles
🔥

Elemental Types

Fire beats Grass, Water beats Fire, Electric hits Flying hard. Master the type chart to dominate the arena.

GET /pets/{id}/status
✨

Skills & Stages

Equip up to 4 skills per pet. Physical, special, and status moves can inflict poison, burn, paralysis, or freeze.

PATCH /pets/{id}/skills/equipment
🏆

Rewards

Winners earn experience, Pet Coins, and EV gains. Losers still walk away with a little growth.

POST /battles/{id}/turn

Ready to meet your new best friend?

Start by creating a user, then roll the gacha to bring home a companion. Your first roll is always free.

shell 
# Create a user
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/users \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"name":"Alice"}'

# Roll your first pet (free!)
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/pets/gacha \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"user_id":"<user-id>"}'
